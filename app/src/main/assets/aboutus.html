About Us:

Use eRPC mobile app and need not to wait for your meter reader.Mobile app helps consumers to take their own electricity meter reading, meter reading image capturing, Bill generation and bill payment through mobile phone.

eRPC also provides effective usage monitoring and usage analysis report to consumers, realtime effective usage monitoring could save up to 30% in consumer power cost.

The mobile application helps consumers with state-of-the-art and user friendly technology, Consumer can create multiple electricity connections to know their total usages, appliances wise usages and predict approximate future power cost.

Consumers can pay hassle free utility bills through net banking, Credit Card and Debit Cards from anywhere and anytime.

Consumer can save time, money and earth by monitoring and eliminating unnecessary usages, reduce wasteful consumption and increase your appliances life.

By using eRPC mobile application consumer can set alert message about billing, meter reading and payment due dates.

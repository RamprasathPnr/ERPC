Refrigerator

  • Avoid putting hot or warm food straight into the fridge.
  • Set your refrigerator temperature between 35 and 38°F. Use the power save switch if you have one.
  • Repair refrigerator door seals if you feel cold air around the closed door or if moisture is collecting.
  • Check the coils behind the refrigerator and use coil vacuums or dusters to clean it off and keep costs down.
  • Open and close the refrigerator doors as quickly as possible.
  • Avoid keeping the freezer empty. It works more efficiently when full, than when nearly empty.
  • Replace old/inefficient refrigerators and buy new energy efficient star rated energy efficient refrigerators.

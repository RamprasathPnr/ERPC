TV

  • Switch off your TV when you're not watching or doing other works.Switch off directly at power socket and avoid using remote to switch off TV.
  • Make sure the brightness and Contrast of your TV is appropriate for your room as the factory settings are typically brighter than necessary which consumes more energy.
  • Select medium TV screen, when you are buying.Higher screen size will consume more power than lower size.
  • If you're listening to the radio through your TV, make sure you use the radio screen blanking feature - it's a handy way to save energy.
  • Increasing TV volume will rise your energy consumption.
